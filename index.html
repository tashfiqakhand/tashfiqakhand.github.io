Let us tell you about our story

We are a 5 star barber shop that was founded by Tashfiq Akhand in 1945. We started in a small shop helping locals get the haircut of their dreams and then flourished from there. We now own an established shop in 742 Bondray Drive, K1W 9J1, Ottawa, Ontario and are open everyday from 10am to 6pm. If you want to make a reservation through the phone, please call 613-252-9043. Reserve your seat and come on in to have the cut of your life!
